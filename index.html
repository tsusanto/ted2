About Edward Kaigh

Edward Kaigh is a seasoned attorney with a passion for advocating on behalf of clients facing complex legal challenges. With a comprehensive background in real estate, estate planning and trust administration, he brings extensive experience and a proven track record of success to every case he handles.

Legal Experience

Edward Kaigh has 18 years of experience practicing law, specializing in real estate, estate planning and trust administration. He has represented a diverse range of clients, including individuals, businesses, and organizations, in real estate litigation, unlawful detainer actions, property management, real estate contracts and transactions, fair housing law, small and large estate plan drafting, trust administration and probate petitions.

Throughout his career, Edward Kaigh has developed a reputation for his strategic approach, attention to detail, and unwavering commitment to achieving favorable outcomes for his clients. He is known for his ability to navigate complex legal issues, negotiate effectively.

Community Involvement

In addition to his legal practice, Edward is committed to giving back to the community. He regularly volunteers his time and expertise with the Justice & Diversity Center of the Bar Association of San Francisco. In this program, Edward provides pro bono legal services to low-income individuals with family law related matters.

Personal Interests

Outside of his legal practice, Edward Kaigh enjoys spending time with his three children. He is also an avid pickleball player and classic car enthusiast.

Education
